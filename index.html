About our salon...


Located in the beautiful Paardevlei precinct. Polished Cherry is an exclusive Nail and beauty Salon specializing in premium nail services. We were estalished in 2018 and have a team of highly talented and experianced technicians.

We offer professional in-salon and mobile nail care services. Our nail technicians will ensure our clients own nails grow naturally thus promoting strong, healthy, and natural nails. 

Our mission at Polished Cherry is to provide our clients a friendly, personlized service through a team of talented professionals. Teamwork is our most valuale asset which ensures our clients are always numer one.
